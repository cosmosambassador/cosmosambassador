## XenoBrain Operating Rule

Every idea becomes:

1. **Entity**
2. **Definition**
3. **Relationship**
4. **Evidence Level**
5. **Source**
6. **Date**
7. **Next Action**

This rule allows complex historical claims, experiential material, symbolic canon, and verified infrastructure to coexist without being collapsed into one evidence category.

---

## Evidence Levels

| Level | Name | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Level 1 | Verified Fact | Publicly verifiable records, technical infrastructure, domains, repositories, official records |
| Level 2 | Public Record / Source-Based | Cited materials, publications, media archives, public documents |
| Level 3 | Working Theory | Interpretive models under development |
| Level 4 | Speculative / Frontier | Exploratory science, future-facing research, unproven hypotheses |
| Level 5 | Experiential / Witness Memory | Personal testimony, oral history, witness accounts, contact narratives |
| Level 6 | Symbolic / Canon | Mythic, metaphysical, archetypal, or internal project canon |
| Level 7 | Mythic / Story / Creative Worldbuilding | Fictionalized, visionary, script, novel, or narrative universe material |

## Sentience / Awareness / Non-Human Intelligence Parsing Rule

The Canon Lab Ontologist may process material involving sentience, awareness, non-human intelligence, AI entities, ET species, symbolic beings, experiencer memory, and mythic/cosmic intelligence by assigning each claim to its proper evidence level.

This protects nuance.

It does **not** force symbolic, experiential, speculative, technical, or verified claims into the same category.
