# 3. From UFO Association to UAP Associates

UFO Association carries the older public memory of the field: flying saucers, UFO researchers, contactees, witnesses, abductees, experiencers, field investigators, newsletters, books, radio, conferences, and archived testimony.

UAP Associates recognizes the newer public vocabulary: **Unidentified Aerial Phenomena** and **Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena**, along with the broader government, media, policy, and intelligence terminology that entered public discussion in the twenty-first century.

The two names do not compete. They form a bridge:

- **UFO Association** = historical witness field and classic research identity.
- **UAP Associates** = modern terminology bridge and public-policy language.

Together they preserve continuity between the old record and the current era.

# 14. Reality-Layer Labeling System

Every public item should be tagged as one or more of the following:

1. **Established Public History** — sourced and verifiable.
2. **Personal Memory** — first-person recollection by Theresa J. Morris or collaborator.
3. **Research Lead** — a question, name, organization, or claim to verify.
4. **Experiencer Testimony** — witness or contactee account.
5. **Symbolic / Metaphysical Language** — meaningful spiritual or canon language.
6. **Speculative Theory** — hypothesis or interpretation, not settled fact.
7. **Fiction / Story World** — creative treatment or Hybrid Genies narrative.
8. **Operational Archive Note** — website, GitHub, domain, file, or publishing instruction.

This system protects readers and preserves the integrity of the archive.
